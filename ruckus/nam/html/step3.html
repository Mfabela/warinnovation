Your dashboard lets you zoom out for a global view of your
WLAN and zoom in for a closer look.

The tear drops on the map show the location of your venues.
The color of the tear drop represents the status of the venue and if action is required.

What they represent:

  • Requires
    attention

  • Temporarily
    degraded

  • In setup
    phase

  • All
    good

Hover over or click on the tear drop to get more information about the venue.
