To us innovation is relative, innovation does not necessarily mean building a cool app or creating the latest "cool" gadget. What Innovaton means to us is how we can make your life a little better. How we can contribute to make life easier. We do that by looking at the problem and then we find the best solution. Whether we do it through open source technology, green technology, art or a totally new invention, whatever it takes. We want to make innovation affordable for everyone.

We are inspired by Africa. We love everything about Africa. From the music, food to the weather and history . We use African values to create products that speak to Africans. Being African is not just being born in Africa but it also means how you think, speak and play. We aim to create innovative products that will inspire you and speak to you in your language.
